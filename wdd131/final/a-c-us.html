About Us

At Shadow Storm Computers, we began with a vision. Our founder, Colby Wilson, looked at the modern tech landscape and saw an opportunity—to create something better. From that spark, a company was born with a mission to reshape how people experience technology.

Whether it's building custom PCs, performing expert repairs, or offering reliable tech consulting, we believe technology should work for you—seamlessly, powerfully, and reliably. Every service we provide is rooted in that belief.

We specialize in custom-built computers designed to meet your exact needs. Whether you're a gamer seeking top-tier performance, a content creator pushing boundaries, or a business professional needing rock-solid reliability, we craft machines that match your goals.

At our core, we're about more than just computers—we're about empowering people with the tools to thrive in a digital world. Technology is power. We help you harness it.
